We are seeking a Director of Engineering to lead the evolution of our shared platform capabilities that power our Integrated Risk Management and Insurance Product engineering teams. The Platform organization operates as a shared service model, partnering closely with product-aligned engineering teams to deliver scalable, secure, and high-performing platform capabilities that serve the entire enterprise. This role requires strong technical depth, excellent people leadership skills, security awareness, and a forward-looking mindset on AI in the software development lifecycle.

- Define and execute the platform modernization roadmap in alignment with long-term business and technology strategy.
- Deliver shared platform services that enable IRM and Insurance engineering teams to build faster and more consistently.
- Drive architectural improvements across APIs, data, caching, CI/CD, observability, identity, and cloud infrastructure.
- Promote modular, API-driven, and service-oriented design principles.
- Operate the Platform team as a force multiplier for product-aligned engineering teams.
- Partner with IRM and Insurance Product Directors to balance platform enablement with delivery timelines.
- Establish clear SLAs and engagement models for shared capabilities.
- Promote knowledge sharing and cross-team collaboration.
- Lead adoption of AI-assisted engineering practices across the platform organization.
- Establish guardrails for responsible AI usage in code generation, test automation, documentation, and architectural analysis.
- Partner with leadership to define measurable AI productivity and quality metrics.
- Provide leadership, management, and technical vision to the Platform engineering organization.
- Manage managers and senior technical leaders.
- Inspire and motivate teams to deliver innovative solutions with urgency and accountability.
- Lead hiring, performance management, compensation, and career development processes.
- Mentor engineering leaders and foster a culture of design excellence and continuous learning.
- Identify and coordinate technical training opportunities.
- Stimulate knowledge sharing and elevate best practices across teams.
- Embed secure-by-design principles across the platform.
- Partner with Security and Architecture to ensure compliance with industry standards and regulatory expectations.
- Strengthen identity, authentication, authorization, secrets management, and data protection strategies.
