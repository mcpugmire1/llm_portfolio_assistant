Senior Engineering Leader, Product Platform

Company context (fictional): Established enterprise company with a core platform that drives revenue, a growing product portfolio, and an engineering organization that has outgrown its current structure. Leadership recognizes that engineering needs to operate as a strategic function, not a delivery service.


About the role

We're looking for a senior engineering leader to own the build-out and evolution of our product engineering organization. This is not a maintenance role. Our platform serves millions of users in a regulated environment, and we need someone who can modernize it without disrupting the business that depends on it — while simultaneously building the engineering culture and capability to sustain that evolution long-term.

You will report to the CTO and partner directly with Product, Design, and Business leadership. You will own engineering strategy, organizational design, talent development, and delivery outcomes.


What you will own


Lead and scale a cross-functional product engineering organization (150-300 engineers, product managers, and designers)
Define the engineering operating model: hiring philosophy, team structure, delivery practices, quality standards, and career development
Drive platform modernization across a legacy core while maintaining business continuity
Establish AI-assisted engineering practices with guardrails for quality, security, and responsible adoption
Partner with C-suite to translate business strategy into technical roadmap and organizational priorities
Build a culture of sustainable delivery — high throughput without heroics



Required qualifications


10+ years of progressive software engineering experience, including hands-on development earlier in career
5+ years leading engineering managers and senior technical leaders in a multi-team environment
Demonstrated experience building or scaling an engineering organization (from early stage to 100+ or inheriting and restructuring an existing org)
Cloud-native architecture experience (AWS, Azure, or GCP) at the platform level
Track record of legacy platform modernization in production environments without business disruption
Cross-functional leadership experience — engineering, product, and design operating as integrated units
Experience establishing or maturing engineering delivery practices (CI/CD, automated testing, observability, release management)
Regulated industry experience (financial services, healthcare, government, or equivalent complexity)
Demonstrated ability to translate business strategy into engineering roadmap and organizational structure
AI/ML adoption strategy — practical experience introducing AI-assisted development practices into an engineering organization
Modern technology stack fluency across frontend and backend (React or equivalent, Python/Node.js/Go, containerized microservices)
Budget ownership or P&L accountability for an engineering function



Preferred qualifications


Product company experience (SaaS, platform, or marketplace) in addition to or instead of consulting/services background
Healthcare, insurance, or life sciences domain knowledge
Hands-on Kubernetes experience at production scale (cluster management, service mesh, multi-tenant orchestration)
Graduate degree in a technical field (Computer Science, Engineering, Information Technology, or related)
Published thought leadership or conference speaking on engineering culture, modernization, or AI adoption



Location

Multiple office locations. Hybrid or in-person preferred. Open to relocation for the right candidate.


Compensation

Competitive with market for senior engineering leadership. Equity participation included.
